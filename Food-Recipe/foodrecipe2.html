Blueberry Smoothie Bowl

  1. step.1 Blend blueberries, 1/2 banana, water, cashew butter, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth; pour into a bowl.
  2. step.2 Top smoothie with sliced banana, almonds, and coconut.
# Ingredients Amount
1 Frozen blueberrys 2 tbsp.
2 chopped bananas 1 piece
3 spoon of water 1 cup
4 chashew butter 2 tbsp.
5 vanilla extract 2 tbsp
6 sliced almond 1 tbsp
7 shredded unsweetend coconut 1/2 tbsp
